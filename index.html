Questions? We've Got Answers.

Do I need a new phone number?

No. Nicole uses call forwarding from your existing business number. Dial a simple code on your phone and missed calls go to her automatically.

What if Nicole can't answer a question?

She takes a detailed message and texts it to you instantly. Complex quotes, complaints, or out-of-scope jobs are flagged for your personal callback.

Can I change Nicole's voice and personality?

Absolutely. Choose from 4 prebuilt voices (NZ and AU accents). Pro plans let you name her anything — "Sarah", "Mike", whatever fits your brand.

How does the tradie mobile app work?

It's a Progressive Web App — no App Store needed. Visit the URL, tap "Add to Home Screen", done. Works offline, PIN login, see today's jobs with travel times.

What about after-hours and emergency calls?

Nicole detects urgency keywords and offers after-hours rates. True emergencies (electrical hazards, gas leaks) get redirected to Vector, Watercare, or 111 automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no setup fees, no cancellation penalties. If Nicole doesn't pay for herself in the first month, you shouldn't keep her.
